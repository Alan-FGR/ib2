About IB2 Theft Prevention System

A collaborative effort to prevent bicycle theft

Our Goal

At IB2, we are dedicated to redefining bicycle security. Our mission is to offer unrivaled protection for your bicycle, ensuring peace of mind through the most innovative theft prevention strategies available today. We believe that every cyclist deserves to enjoy their ride without the worry of theft, no matter where they are.

Who We Are

Founded by a team of passionate cyclists and security experts, IB2 Theft Prevention System emerged from a desire to solve a personal problem that affects millions worldwide: bicycle theft. With years of research and development, we have crafted a multi-layered security system that addresses all aspects of bicycle protection.

Our System

Our system incorporates a blend of robust physical security measures and cutting-edge technology. From high-security U-locks certified by Sold Secure Gold to advanced "DNA"-marking and GPS tracking, we ensure your bike remains secure. Our system also includes:

  • Immediate Alerts: Automated notifications that are immediately forwarded to local law enforcement upon theft attempts.
  • Custom Modifications: From discrete "IB2" stickers to custom paints and engravings, we provide options that make your bike uniquely secure.
  • Preventative Advice: Guidance on proper bike locking techniques and maintaining a low theft profile.

Why Use IB2?

  1. Comprehensive Protection: A multi-tiered approach that covers physical locks, technological tracking, and proactive theft deterrence measures.
  2. Innovative Features: We continually evolve our services to include the latest in theft prevention technology, like high-security locks and hidden cameras, ensuring that our system adapts to new challenges.
  3. Community and Support: We don't just protect bikes; we support the cycling community. We offer advice, help recover stolen bikes, and work continuously to improve urban cycling safety.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow and refine our system, IB2 remains committed to being a leader in effective theft prevention solutions. We are constantly exploring new technologies and strategies to keep your bike safe, as we expand our reach to cyclists around the globe.

For more information on how IB2 can protect your bike, visit our System Page. Let us keep our bikes safe!
