The IB2 Theft Prevention System consists of many layers of protection:

Basic protection:
  • Multiple Sold Secure Gold U-locks
  • Bicycle Registration
  • "DNA"-marking
  • GPS tracking
  • Alarm of at least 100db
  • Immediate alert with police forwarding
  • Apply your IB2 stickers in all parts
  • Paint over the brand if it's a recognizable one
  • Take plenty of photos before and after modifications
  • Take clearly readable photos of all serial numbers
  • Keep your invoice/receipt safely stored, make a digital copy
Preventative measures
  • Lock your bike correctly, preferably with the frame and both wheels tightly secured to a fixed object.
  • Keep the market value low. We don't recommend leaving bicycles that can be sold for more than 200 euros locked on the street.
  • Always remove the battery and seat to keep the market value down.
Additional protection:
  • Paint/engrave your IB2 number and QR code in all parts
  • Add custom stickers and painting (hundreds), especially in unreachable surfaces
  • Replace stock parts by cheaper parts
  • Use Pitlock or Hexlock on the wheels, seatpost and stem
  • Install custom and unique spokes and tires
Jurisdiction-dependent (check legality in your country, use at your own risk):
  • "Skunk" locks (that release gas when broken)
  • Hidden camera recording
  • "Active" remote theft prevention mechanisms
